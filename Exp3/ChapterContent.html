Chapter 1: The lone Poltergeist

There once was a ghost, haunting a big castle on a cliff side. He was living a lonely life.


















































Chapter 2: Intruders

One day the poltergeist found that a small family has moved into the castle. He was very happy. He planned to play a little prank on them to introduce himself.













































Chapter 3: The Prank

He waited for the night to come. When the midnight struck, he started moving the curtains, turning the lights on and off, making scary yet goofy noises. The whole family got scared and moves out.

































































Chapter 4: Lonely Again

He just wanted to make a good funny impression but now he is alone in the huge castle again. He was floating there, thinking by himself, "How many families has it been so far....". But he has not lost hope yet, waiting for new family again.
